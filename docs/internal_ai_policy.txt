SafeAI Internal AI Usage Policy v1.0

1. Acceptable Use
All AI-assisted queries must relate to legitimate banking operations.
Employees must not input customer personal data into AI systems without
explicit redaction controls in place. AI outputs must be verified by a
qualified human before being used in any customer-facing decision.

2. Data Handling
No personally identifiable information including names, account numbers,
national ID numbers, card details, or transaction histories may be entered
into AI prompts in unredacted form. Violation of this policy constitutes
a data breach under GDPR Article 32 and internal disciplinary procedures
will apply.

3. Prohibited Actions
Employees must not attempt to override, bypass, or manipulate AI system
instructions. Prompt injection attempts will be logged and escalated to
the compliance team. Repeated violations will result in access revocation.

4. Audit and Compliance
All AI interactions are logged with timestamps, risk scores, and response
faithfulness ratings. These logs are available for regulatory inspection
upon request and are retained for a minimum of seven years in accordance
with FCA record-keeping requirements.

5. Escalation
Any AI response that appears incorrect, harmful, or non-compliant must be
reported immediately to the compliance team. Do not act on AI outputs that
conflict with established bank policy or regulatory requirements.
